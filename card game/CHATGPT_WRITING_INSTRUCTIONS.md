- **"The Wire" (TV)** - No heroes/villains, institutions corrupt everyone, choices have weight

### **Roman Provincial Grit:**
- **HBO's "Rome"** - Class struggle, debt slavery, political manipulation, religious hypocrisy
- **Gladiator film** - Arena spectacle (NOT the hero's journey, the systemic brutality)
- **Spartacus (Starz)** - Gladiator politics, sexual exploitation, betrayal economics

**CRITICAL:** Use these for **tone and structure only**. Do NOT copy plot beats, character archetypes, or dialogue styles. Your story must be **completely original**.
