### **S01: "Deserter's Justice"**
**TITLE:** Marcus's Past
**CATEGORY:** Companion (Marcus the Veteran)
**UNLOCK:** After recruiting Marcus (M01) and clearing M02
**AVAILABILITY WINDOW:** M03-M06 (before Ridge City arc)

**PREMISE:**
Marcus's former legion commander, Centurion Soren, arrives in Arena City with imperial soldiers. He's hunting Marcus for desertion‚ÄîMarcus fled the legions to avoid orders to massacre civilians. Soren wants Marcus arrested for treason. You must choose: surrender Marcus (lose companion, gain favor with state), fight Soren (keep Marcus, gain cult/rebel reputation), or negotiate a secret arrangement.

**PLAYER CHOICES:**

**Choice A: Surrender Marcus**
- Marcus is taken by soldiers
- +3 FAVOR with Governor (state appreciates loyalty)
- +1 HEAT (cooperating with authority noted by enemies)
- -5 Marcus loyalty (permanently, he's gone)
- Reward: 150g (bounty payment from Governor)
- Story consequence: Marcus abandons squad, won't recruit again in Season 1

**Choice B: Fight Soren**
- Centurion Soren + 3 imperial soldiers (challenging fight)
- Victory: Marcus saved, Soren defeated/flees
  - +2 Marcus loyalty (you proved your commitment)
  - +2 HEAT (killed imperial officer, now hunted)
  - -2 FAVOR (killed state official)
  - Unlock Marcus signature cards (Rare tier)
  - Reward: 100g + rare armor (looted from Soren)

- Defeat/Retreat: Soren captures Marcus, you escape
  - Marcus imprisoned (temporary, needs rescue in later mission)
  - +1 HEAT (fled from imperial forces)
  - -3 Marcus loyalty (he's captured because of you)
  - Must complete rescue mission (M09+) to get him back

**Choice C: Negotiate**
- Secret meeting with Soren (he's conflicted about orders)
- Soren: "I don't want to kill Marcus. I want to save him."
- Bribe Soren (+200g payment): He disappears, reports Marcus dead
  - +2 Marcus loyalty (you saved him quietly)
  - +0 HEAT (no violence, no witnesses)
  - +1 FAVOR secretly (Soren owes you, becomes minor ally)
  - Reward: No direct gold, but Soren later provides intel

**POST-MISSION OUTCOMES:**

**If Marcus Stays (Choice B or C success):**
- Marcus becomes loyal, available for M03-M15
- New dialogue: "You stood for me when it would've been easier not to."
- Fights harder in squad (+5% accuracy with Marcus)
- Unlock Marcus companion arc completion in Season 2 (S16)

**If Marcus Leaves (Choice A):**
- Squad loses Marcus permanently until rescue (if ever)
- Recruit replacement lieutenant (Titus, Julia, Corvus)
- Arc unresolved, can be continued in S16 (rescue mission)

**MECHANICAL OUTPUTS:**
- **Unlock:** M01-M02 requirement
- **Cards Unlocked:** Marcus signature cards (if kept/fought for)
- **Gold Reward:** 0-200g (based on choice)
- **Relationship Impact:** Marcus loyalty +2/-5/+1 (branches)
- **Meter Impact:** FAVOR/HEAT variable
- **Difficulty:** ‚≠ê‚≠ê (Centurion is skilled)

**STORY NOTES:**
- Companion missions are **optional but strongly encouraged**
- Skipping S01 = Marcus stays but lower loyalty (-1 default)
- Marcus's loyalty carries to Season 2 companion arc (S16)
- If Marcus killed/captured here, S16 becomes "rescue" mission instead of "advancement"

### **S02: "The Rite Demands"**
**TITLE:** Livia's Initiation
**CATEGORY:** Companion (Livia the Cultist)
**UNLOCK:** After recruiting Livia (M04) and clearing M05
**AVAILABILITY WINDOW:** M06-M08 (Ridge City exploration)

- +1 HEAT (murder witnessed by informants)

- Risk: Cult executes prisoner anyway, blames you for hesitation
- Reward: No blood on hands, clear conscience

- Priestess accepts the rite (hidden doubts)
- +1 PIETY (appears devoted but cult senses fakery)
- +1 Livia loyalty (she appreciates the compromise)
- -1 FAVOR (prisoner escapes, later causes problems for authorities)

**POST-MISSION OUTCOMES:**

- Livia fully trusts you, strongest bond
- Cult sees you as zealot, treats you with respect
- Later missions with cult (M12, S11) give bonuses
- Companion arc (S16) involves ascending cult hierarchy

**If Refusal (Choice B):**
- Livia conflicted but respects your integrity
- Cult remains suspicious, future missions harder
- If loyalty stays >2, relationship salvageable in S16
- If loyalty drops <2, Livia abandons after M08

**If Fake (Choice C):**
- Livia becomes secret ally (you share deception)
- Priestess suspicious but can't prove it
- Creates hidden hook for future revelation
- S16 can explore deception consequences or continuation

**MECHANICAL OUTPUTS:**

### **S03: "Debt Collector's Due"**
**TITLE:** Titus's Creditors
**CATEGORY:** Companion (Titus the Noble)
**UNLOCK:** After recruiting Titus (M06-M08) and clearing M08
**AVAILABILITY WINDOW:** M08-M11 (Ridge City/Port City transition)

Titus is terrified. You can: pay the debt (costly), fight the creditors (dangerous), convince Titus to work with you for profit (exploit him), or help him disappear (lose lieutenant temporarily).

**Choice A: Pay Titus's Debt**
- You pay 300g to merchant
- Titus +3 loyalty (you saved him from servitude)
- Merchant satisfied, leaves town
- Titus "Connected" trait remains active (shop prices -10%)
- Reward: Titus becomes permanent loyal member, +1 FAVOR (state appreciates debts cleared)
- Cost: -300g (significant gold loss)

**Choice B: Fight the Creditors**
- Combat vs. merchant + 2 wealthy bodyguards (moderate difficulty)
- Victory: Creditors killed/flee
  - Titus +1 loyalty (you fought for him, but messy)
  - +2 HEAT (killed civilians, investigation)
  - +2 DEBT (you owe Titus now)
  - Reward: 150g looted from merchant
  - Consequence: Titus indebted to you, creates power dynamic

- Defeat/Retreat: Creditors capture Titus, you escape
  - Titus imprisoned/indentured
  - Must rescue him later (additional mission)

**Choice C: Exploit Titus's Fear**
- You demand Titus work for you to pay debt
- Titus agrees under duress
- +2 DEBT (Titus owes you, creates resentment)
- -1 Titus loyalty (he resents manipulation)
- +200g (he steals from his creditor to pay you)
- Reward: Titus continues fighting but distrustful
- Risk: S16 he may betray you if offered freedom

**Choice D: Help Him Disappear**
- You assist Titus in faking his death/fleeing
- Creditor believes Titus dead, abandons search
- Titus leaves squad temporarily (goes into hiding)
- +0 loyalty (grateful but absent)
- +1 PIETY if cult helps hide him
- Titus returns later (M11-M15) as hidden contact
- Reward: No immediate cost, but Titus unavailable for M09-M10

**POST-MISSION OUTCOMES:**

**If Debt Paid (Choice A):**
- Titus becomes loyal ally
- "Connected" trait fully activated (best prices in region)
- Companion arc (S16) involves Titus building political power
- Stable, trusting relationship

**If Fight (Choice B):**
- Titus grateful but cautious
- Relationship based on shared violence, not trust
- S16 can explore mutual respect or emerging conflict
- Titus more combat-focused, less politically useful

**If Exploit (Choice C):**
- Titus becomes resentful servant
- Fights with you but distrusts you
- S16 choice point: betray him or prove yourself trustworthy
- Creates dramatic potential

**If Disappear (Choice D):**
- Titus available as hidden informant
- Returns later with valuable intelligence
- "Connected" trait becomes passive (underground network instead of shops)
- Mystery/espionage focus
